2024 edition

On January 31, 2024, the first edition of the Jeanne d’HACK CTF took place at the University of Rouen. This competition tested the skills of students through a series of varied challenges. Participants had the opportunity to compete in challenges related to cryptography, OSINT, Pwn, reverse engineering, web, Windows, and hardware!

For those who wish to explore further, the GitHub for the CTF is available at this address.

We would like to express our sincere thanks to all the teams that participated in this event, to the teachers who helped us organize the CTF, and to all the members of the association.
